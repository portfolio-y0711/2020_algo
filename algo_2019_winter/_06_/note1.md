### 풀이

##### 예제 1

```ts
[[yellow_hat, headgear], [blue_sunglasses, eyewear], [green_turban, headgear]]

{ "headgear": 2, "eyewear": 1}

3 * 2 - 1 = 5

```

##### 예제 2
```ts
[[crow_mask, face], [blue_sunglasses, face], [smoky_makeup, face]]

{ "face": 3 }

4 - 1 = 3

```



##### 예제 3

```ts
[["abc" , "headgear"], [], [], [], [], [], [], [], [], []]

{ "headgear": 3, "top": 2, "bottom": 1}

4 * 3 * 2 - 1 = 23

```
